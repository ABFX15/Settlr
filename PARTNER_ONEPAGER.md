## What we are
Offbank is the settlement layer for cannabis B2B payments. Licensed dispensaries,
wholesalers, and distributors invoice and pay each other in USDC — instant,
final, and on a rail no processor can freeze. We are **non-custodial**: merchants
hold their own funds in a Squads multisig vault; we never take custody. We run
KYB, AML, and a full cryptographic audit trail on every dollar.
