Recommendation

For funding: lead with the wedge, not the chain. Pitch = "Non-custodial USDC settlement for high-risk & crypto-native B2B — beachhead in [reachable vertical], expansion to cannabis/CBD/firearms as banking access stays broken." Keep the architecture chain-agnostic internally (your DB/pipeline already is) so "multi-chain" is a credible roadmap slide, never a current rebuild.

Do

  • Get 1 real settled payment from a reachable buyer (crypto-native B2B / agency / DAO payout). One live tx on X with the Solscan link is worth more to an investor than any deck slide.
  • Position cannabis as the wedge story, not the whole TAM — "narrow beachhead, obvious land-and-expand" beats "we do all payments."
  • Lead the moat: compliance + off-ramp (Sphere) + Cloak privacy. That's the defensible part.
  • Research the one real chain signal: is there USDT/Tron pull in international wholesale? Only expand if a buyer pulls you.

Don't

  • Don't go multi-chain to look bigger. Reads as unfocused; dilutes the "Solana speed + non-custodial" wedge; triples your surface for zero new buyers.
  • Don't resume cold-DMing cannabis distributors. The 100-attempt experiment already returned its result.
  • Don't raise on "the market is huge." TAM masks the absence of a single paying user — investors will see through it.
