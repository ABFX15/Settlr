# Offbank — in plain English

## The problem

Cannabis is legal in most states, but **banks still won't touch it.** Dispensaries
and distributors get their accounts frozen or closed with no warning, get charged
8–12% by predatory processors, and end up running on cash — which is dangerous,
expensive, and impossible to audit.

When a payment processor goes down (it happened in March 2026), these businesses
lose days of revenue overnight. They have no safety net.

## What Offbank is

**A way for cannabis businesses to get paid and pay each other in digital dollars
that no bank can freeze.**

Those digital dollars are called USDC. Think of one as a dollar that lives on the
internet instead of in a bank account — it's always worth exactly $1, it moves in
seconds, and because it isn't sitting *in* a bank, no bank can switch it off.

So a dispensary can:
- send an invoice and get paid **instantly**,
- pay its suppliers and staff,
- and hold its money safely —

all without depending on a bank that might cut them off tomorrow.

## How it works (the three steps)

1. **Get paid.** A buyer pays an invoice — either from their own digital-dollar
   wallet, or with a normal debit card / bank transfer (we convert it for them).
   The money lands in the merchant's own wallet. They control it; we never hold it.

2. **Use it.** The merchant pays suppliers and other businesses directly in digital
   dollars — instant, final, no bank involved. The more businesses on the network,
   the more they can just pay each other and never cash out at all.

3. **Cash out (only when needed).** For payroll, rent, or taxes they need real bank
   dollars. We convert their digital dollars to USD and send it to a **special
   cannabis-friendly bank** that's happy to hold it — with complete paperwork
   proving the money came from licensed, legal sales.

## Why it's safe (and legal)

- **The money mostly lives as digital dollars — which can't be frozen.** That's the
  protection. A business only converts to bank dollars for the small amount it
  actually needs as cash.
- **When it does cash out, it goes to a bank that wants cannabis business** — not a
  normal bank that will panic.
- **We win that bank over with honesty, not tricks.** Every payout comes with a full
  record: who's licensed, where every dollar came from, verified on a public ledger.
  We over-explain so the bank stays comfortable. (Hiding the source would be illegal;
  proving it is the whole point.)

## Why it's better than today

| Today | With Offbank |
|---|---|
| Bank closes you for being "high-risk" | Your money can't be frozen |
| Processors charge 8–12% + hold reserves | 1% flat, no reserves |
| Wires take 3–5 days | Settles in seconds |
| Cash-heavy, fails audits | Every dollar traceable on-chain |
| One outage = days of lost revenue | Always-on, no single point of failure |

## The business in one line

**Offbank is freeze-proof digital-dollar payments for cannabis (and other
banks-won't-serve industries) — get paid, pay each other, and cash out to a
cannabis-friendly bank only when you need to, with airtight compliance built in.**

The bigger the network grows, the less anyone needs to cash out — because they can
just pay each other in digital dollars. That's the moat a bank can never copy.
